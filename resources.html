Cannabis for Medical Use

As a registered member you will have access to a wide selection of indica, sativa, hybrid and CBD-rich strains, in the form of both dried flower and cannabis oil. In order to provide members with a treatment that best meets their unique needs and requirements, we offer a variety of options for flexible, effective relief. We hope you will come to trust our product selection, consistency, and availability. Every time you order a new strain from us a strain information card will be sent with your order. These strain cards list a detailed breakdown of the strain, THC and CBD content, anecdotal effects, as well as terpene and flavour profiles, to help you find the strains that are right for you.

Product offering

We grow a variety of strains in our state-of-the-art, pharmaceutical-grade facility. Currently, we offer a carefully cultivated selection of premium indica, sativa, and hybrid strains which our registered members can access via our online shop.

Register

To access and purchase cannabis with Kolab Project, you must first sign up and become registered. Please follow the registration steps outlined below. If you have questions or concerns, our team is here to help you.

1. Create an Account

Sign up for your Kolab Project account.

2. Complete Our Registration Form

Fill out the registration form online. If you choose to print it, simply bring it to your health care practitioner along with the medical document below.

3. Submit a Medical Document

Download the medical document and take it to your healthcare provider. They will need to send it to us via secure fax or mail.

4. Welcome to Kolab Project

Once your registration is processed and approved by our team, you will receive your welcome package and you can place your orders.
